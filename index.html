This Gun for Hire

Howdy, I’m Richard Sime, a front-end developer. I've been wrangling websites for ten years – you’re probably familiar with my work.

Having worked for some of Wellington’s top design agencies, I have helped build sites for Kiwibank, Wellington Airport, NZ International Arts Festival, CreativeNZ, Chamber Music, the University of New South Wales and a number of other high profile clients. Take a gander at my portfolio.

If you're a design agency or development company needing another hand on deck to get you through a busy spell I can help. If you're a potential client requiring a new website or just a refresh then give me a holler. I specialise in responsive design, and can perform wonders on an old school site that needs to go mobile.

My skillset includes CSS (OO SASS), HTML, JavaScript, RWD, Adobe CS, &c. I also have access to a small bunch of super talented designers and programmers to get end-to-end projects done.
